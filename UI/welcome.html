Welcome to LedgerSMB

What is LedgerSMB

LedgerSMB is a double entry accounting system offering a wide variety of related functionalities, such as

  • invoicing
  • bank reconciliation
  • ordering
  • keeping stock
  • tracking hours (timecards)
  • shipping & receiving
  • tracking of unpaid invoices

Contributing

The project is always looking for contributions. The easiest ways to contribute to the project are:

  • Report your bugs and problems to the project's GitHub bug tracker or mailing lists
  • Help translate the software through the LedgerSMB project Transifex web-based translation project
  • Fix a bug and submit a "Pull Request"
    When you do, please start with one marked 'bite-sized' and contact the development team through IRC and/or matrix for help getting started.

Notice: SourceForge project closed down

Due to loss of control over some parts of the project's hosting infrastructure on SourceForge, the project decided to close down its SourceForge infrastructure. Mailing lists have moved to self-hosted mailing list infrastructure.
